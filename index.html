About Our Trips

We specialize in taking adventurous monkeys on unforgettable journeys into the cosmos. Our state of the art spacecrafts are equipped with all the bananas you can eat and space suits tailored to fit your cowboy hats and boots.

Our Packages

  • Lunar Lasso Adventure: Swing by the moon and practice your lassoing skills in low gravity.
  • Martian Rodeo: Experience the red planet and test your rodeo talents against Martian dust devils.
  • Galactic Roundup: A grand tour of the Milky Way, rounding up stars and planets as souvenirs.
